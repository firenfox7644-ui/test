Note For Approval 
Date	26 May 2026
Subject	Approval to onboard Plotline as In-App Engagement & Personalization Platform Partner
Final Price, Terms, Contract Period etc.	Commercials to be updated by Procurement team
Lock in: To be finalized as per agreement
Contract Term: 3 Years
Preface & Key Points	To onboard a strategic In-App Engagement & Personalization Platform partner for IndiGo Digital ecosystem to strengthen customer engagement, improve app conversion journeys, enable contextual personalization, increase ancillary adoption, and reduce engineering dependency for customer communication and campaign execution across mobile applications.
Vendor/OEM	Plotline (Commercial Ratings – L2 and Technical Ratings – T1)
Process followed / Price justification / Benchmark / Exception	RFP process was followed to identify a suitable In-App Engagement & Personalization Platform partner for IndiGo App ecosystem. Three vendors participated in the RFP and were evaluated across Technical, Functional, Product Capability, Operational, and InfoSec parameters.
Technical and commercial rankings were captured as below:

Vendor Name	Commercial Ratings	Technical Ratings
Pushwoosh	L1	T3
Plot Line	L2	T1
Purpletalk	L3	T2

Plotline was assigned the highest technical rating (T1) among participating vendors based on:
• Superior contextual in-app engagement capability aligned to airline customer journeys
• Strong personalization and journey orchestration capability
• Better fitment for real-time operational notifications and dynamic customer interactions
• Stronger low-code/no-code campaign execution capability reducing engineering dependency

Although Pushwoosh emerged as the L1 vendor commercially, Digital team recommends proceeding with Plotline considering overall strategic product capability, long-term scalability, operational fitment, and future Digital Experience roadmap alignment.

From an operational perspective, the proposed platform is expected to:
• Improve direct customer engagement and booking conversion opportunities
• Enable faster campaign rollout without release dependency
• Reduce turnaround time for customer targeting and personalization initiatives
• Improve ancillary upsell and contextual engagement opportunities
• Strengthen future AI-led and behavior-driven engagement use cases across IndiGo Digital platforms
Commercial comparison and negotiated commercials shall be updated by Procurement team in the final version of NFA.
What are the budgetary allocations	The proposed platform cost shall be funded through approved Digital platform and customer engagement budget allocation for FY 2026-27.
 Vendor Justification	Plotline demonstrated stronger product maturity, contextual engagement capability, operational scalability, and Digital Experience alignment compared to other participating vendors. The platform provides better long-term strategic fitment for IndiGo’s evolving customer engagement and personalization ecosystem.
Exception on which approval is requested (Vendor Justification)	Despite being the second-lowest cost bidder (L2), Plotline is recommended as the preferred partner due to its superior technical rating (T1), stronger Digital Experience capability, operational scalability, better future readiness, and overall alignment with IndiGo’s long-term customer engagement and personalization strategy. Digital team recommends proceeding with Plotline considering the overall business and operational advantage over the L1 vendor.
