Additionally, here is a breakdown of large-scale enterprises in India and the global airline/travel industry that rely on these platforms to drive growth and operational agility.
Travel and aviation, in particular, rely heavily on these tools due to the complexity of their funnels (flight selection, ancillaries, seat maps, multi-passenger inputs) and the need for high-frequency transactional messaging.

1. MoEngage (MarComm, Orchestration & Engagement)
Because MoEngage was built primarily for mobile-first engagement with complex triggered journeys, it has massive penetration in the Indian travel and hospitality sector.
•	Airlines & Travel: Cleartrip (uses MoEngage extensively to identify booking funnel drop-offs and trigger automated smart campaigns), MakeMyTrip, OYO, and Treebo.
•	Other Indian Enterprises: Flipkart, BigBasket, ShareChat, and Domino’s India.

2. Amplitude (Product Analytics & Funnel Optimization)
Amplitude is the industry standard for mapping out highly complex user paths, which is why product-led growth teams favor it over traditional marketing analytics.
•	Airlines & Travel: Air France / KLM uses Amplitude globally to understand passenger digital behavior across web and app. In the broader Indian travel-tech ecosystem, players like MakeMyTrip heavily integrate advanced analytics stacks to map complex multi-session travel bookings.
•	Other Indian Enterprises: Swiggy, Dream11, Meesho, and Acko.

3. Insider (Web Personalization & CRO)
Insider’s strength lies in its deep web personalization, dynamic overlays, and unified profile management, making it popular for brands looking to uplift direct-to-consumer web conversions.
•	Airlines & Travel: Singapore Airlines and Pegasus Airlines use Insider for cross-channel orchestration and web-based personalization (e.g., dynamically changing the website layout based on a user's loyalty tier).
•	Other Indian Enterprises: BigBasket, Puma India, MAC, and Marks & Spencer India.

4. VWO (Experimentation & A/B Testing)
VWO (Visual Website Optimizer) is an Indian-born global company, so its footprint in large Indian digital enterprises is vast.
•	Airlines & Travel: MakeMyTrip is one of the most prominent users of VWO in the travel sector, running constant A/B/N tests on flight search results pages, hotel detail pages, and checkout flows.
•	Other Indian Enterprises: Tata 1mg, Paytm, and Pepperfry.

Strategic Observation for a High-Growth Marketplace: 
Notice how travel aggregators like MakeMyTrip and Cleartrip do not rely on a monolithic suite like Adobe. Because their margin heavily depends on conversion rates and cross-selling ancillaries (hotels, cabs, insurance), they utilize a Composable Stack. They plug tools like MoEngage (for triggered drop-off emails/push) and VWO (for testing the UI of the booking page) into their central data layer to move much faster than legacy systems allow.
